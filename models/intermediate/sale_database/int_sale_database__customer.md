{% docs int_sale_database__customer %}

This model provides a summary of customer activity, focusing on their order history and purchasing behavior. It aggregates key metrics for each customer, such as:

- **Total Amount Spent**: The total amount spent by the customer.
- **Total Items**: The total quantity of items purchased by the customer across all orders.
- **Total Distinct Items**: The count of distinct product types purchased by the customer.
- **Total Orders**: The total number of orders placed by the customer.
- **Favorite Product**: The product most frequently purchased by the customer, based on the quantity of items ordered.
